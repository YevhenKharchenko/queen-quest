Game Overview

Enter a world where magic and logic collide in Queen Quest – a vibrant match-3 puzzle game filled with glittering jewels and exciting challenges. Travel through enchanted lands, solve puzzles, and uncover royal secrets as you build your path to the throne. Whether you're a casual player or a match-3 master, the kingdom awaits your strategy and sparkle.

Gameplay Highlights

  • Hundreds of Levels

    Every level is packed with twists, goals, and surprises.

  • Royal Boosters

    Use powerful items to clear tricky spots.

  • Evolving Map

    Unlock charming new regions as you advance.

  • Daily Spin & Events

    Win rewards, boosters, and more each day!

  • Creative Challenges

    More than just matching: clear fog, break chains, and collect crowns.

Characters

  • Queen Lyra

    Wise and kind ruler of the Puzzle Kingdom. Helps guide your journey and offers tips.

  • Sir Twinkle

    A mischievous court wizard who introduces magical boosters.

  • Lady Gemina

    Keeper of the Gem Towers and your ally in timed challenges.

  • Lord Gloom

    The sneaky villain trying to fog up your path with tricky puzzles.

How It Works

Match 3 or more jewels to clear the board

Complete unique objectives in each level (collect crowns, clear fog, free gems)

Use boosters like the Royal Wand and Crown Bomb to turn the tide

Unlock and explore magical lands as you win stars
